About Us

Packapeer Academy is founded in 2019 by Nikhil Kilivayil along with his brother Sibi Kilivayil with an aim to help millions of students across the globe to improve their standards of living by educating them with industry relevant skills. The Academy has started in a small district of Calicut with less than 12 students and has grown into a reputable institution in the hearts of millions of people across Kerala. Packapeer Academy has operations in 3 major cities of Kerala i.e Calicut, Kochi and Trivandrum and has impacted hundreds of students as of now.
